Why I Chose UMD

Outdoor Recreation Opportunities

I have been able to participate in the outdoors here like no other place. I am able to ski, hike, and climb.

Ability to get Involved

With being a medium sized college, the opportunity for leadership is much greater than other universities. I have taken advantage of this by becoming th Vice President of Accounting Club and the Treasurer for the UMD Lacrosse Team.

The Business School

Labovitz is a very strong school that has great connections for post grad opportunities. I really admire the flexibilty and tight-knit community.

What I Love About Duluth

Duluth is a phenomenal place to start my life as it has every amenity that you need from a big city but it also has a very small town feel. I love how the city is so obsessed with nature and the outdoors. Whether it’s watching the massive lakers pass under the Aerial Lift Bridge or hiking the rugged trails of Magney-Snively, there is a constant sense of wonder right in your backyard. The community's shared passion for the North Shore creates a welcoming environment that makes a newcomer feel like a local in no time.
